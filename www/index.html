Paul the Octopus was an octopus who supposedly predicted the results of soccer matches. He made many accurate predictions which brought him worldwide attention as an animal oracle.
During divinations, Paul's keepers would present him with two boxes containing food. Each box was decorated with the different team flags of the competitors of an upcoming football match. Whichever box Paul ate from first would be considered his prediction for which team would win the match.
